Hello People! Mental Health is as important for a human being as its physical health. Do not ignore your mental health.If facing serious issues for long time please consider a mental health expert doctor for further assisstance.
Schizophrenia

Schizophrenia is like a glitch in the brain that messes up how a person thinks, feels, and acts. It can make them see or hear things that others don't, and sometimes their thoughts get all tangled up. It's like having a confusing and unpredictable mental movie playing in your head.

Severe Depression

Severe depression is like being stuck in a deep, dark pit where everything feels heavy, and even simple tasks seem impossible. It's like having a constant rain cloud over your head, making it hard to find joy in things you used to enjoy.

Anxiety

Anxiety is a conditon where it feels like your brain hits the "worry" button, and it won't stop sending you notifications about things that may or may not happen. It's basically your mind playing the worst-case scenario on repeat, even if the situation isn't as bad as it seems.

Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder is like having a mental roller coaster ride. Sometimes you're zooming up with lots of energy and excitement , and other times you're plummeting down feeling really low and down . It's like your mood has a mind of its own and keeps switching between extreme ups and downs.

Post Traumatic Stress Disorder(PTSD)

PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder) is a conditon where your brain feels like getting stuck on a really scary or upsetting memory, and it keeps replaying, making it hard to move on. It's like having a mental replay button that brings back distressing experiences, even when you don't want it to.

Neurodevelopmental Disorder

A neurodevelopmental disorder is like a hiccup in the brain's usual growth and development. It can affect how someone's brain and nervous system develop, leading to differences in things like learning, behavior, and social skills. It's basically a unique wiring job in the brain that can bring both challenges and strengths.

Eating Disorder

Imagine your brain's relationship with food is like a rollercoaster. Sometimes, it's all about controlling what you eat, like wanting to eat very little or none at all. Other times, it's like your brain's telling you to eat a lot, way more than your body needs . And then there's this other one where someone feels like they gotta throw up after eating, even though they don't want to.

Mood Disorder

Mood disorders are when your feelings are all over the place, like a rollercoaster ride for your emotions. Imagine one day you're super happy, feeling like you're on top of the world, and then the next day, you're feeling really down, like everything's just bleh.

Personality Disorder.

Personality disorders are like patterns in how people think, feel, and behave that are kinda different from what's considered normal. It's like everyone's got their own unique way of seeing the world and reacting to stuff, right? But with personality disorders, these ways of thinking and acting can cause problems in their relationships and daily life.
